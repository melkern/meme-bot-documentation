User Documentation: meme search bot (@hsememorubot)

Your personal assistant for finding memes

What is this bot?

@hsememorubot is your personal assistant for finding memes. Describe what you remember about the meme, and the bot will try to find it for you.

How to use it? Step-by-step guide:

1
Starting the Bot

Find the bot in Telegram and press the START button.

2
Main Menu

After starting, the bot will present you with two buttons:

  • начинаем поиск - to begin a new meme search
  • помогите, не могу разобраться с ботом - to get help (view these instructions).

3
The Search Process

  • Click the начинаем поиск button
  • The bot will respond with: "супер! введи слова для поиска мема"
  • Type words associated with the meme into the chat (e.g., кот в очках, сын маминой подруги)

4
Reviewing and Rating Results

The bot will send the first photo it finds, along with two buttons:

  • ок - if this is the meme you were looking for
  • не ок - if it is not the right one

5
What Happens After You Make a Choice?

If you press не ок:
  • The current photo disappears
  • The bot sends a new photo with the same ок/не ок buttons
  • The bot has a maximum of 3 attempts to find the right meme. If you press не ок three times in a row, the search for your query ends and you will get the message "поиск завершен, попробуй другие слова"
If you press ок:
  • The photo remains in the chat
  • ураааа, нашли! button appears
  • After clicking it, the bot will ask: "хочешь найти ещё один мем?" (offering to return you to step 2)
